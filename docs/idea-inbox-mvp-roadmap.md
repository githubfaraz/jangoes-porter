# Jangoes - Idea Inbox & MVP Roadmap

## 🚀 Vision
Jangoes is a premium, AI-powered logistics platform tailored for the Indian market. It features a high-vibrancy "Liquid Glass" interface that feels modern and approachable. The platform bridges the gap between complex shipping requirements and seamless user experience by leveraging multi-modal AI for parcel classification and precise Google Maps grounding for routing.

## ✨ Current MVP Status (What Works)
- **Vibrant Brand Identity**: Light-themed, aero-mesh background with high-end glass-morphic components.
- **Multi-modal Parcel Analysis**: Users can describe or photograph items. Gemini 3 Flash classifies the category, detects fragility, and estimates weight.
- **Intelligent Routing**: Integration with Google Maps grounding for real-time place searching and address verification.
- **Dynamic Payment Logic**: Automated wallet-first payment system that locks the payment method if the balance is sufficient.
- **Chargeable Weight Engine**: Sophisticated calculation comparing actual weight vs volumetric weight to determine shipping costs.
- **Driver Ecosystem**: Complete flow from registration (KYC/Assets) to active trip management and earnings payouts.
- **AI Support**: A dedicated Gemini-powered support assistant to answer logistics-related queries.
